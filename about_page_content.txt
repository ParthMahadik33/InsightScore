ABOUT INSIGHTSCORE
================================================================================

Welcome to InsightScore - Your trusted partner in understanding creditworthiness through innovative AI-powered financial analysis.

InsightScore is a revolutionary FinTech platform that combines traditional credit bureau scores (CIBIL) with advanced behavioral analysis to provide you with a comprehensive, accurate, and fair credit assessment. We believe that your creditworthiness should reflect not just your past credit history, but also your current financial behavior and discipline.

================================================================================
OUR MISSION
================================================================================

To democratize credit assessment by providing transparent, fair, and comprehensive credit scoring that goes beyond traditional metrics. We aim to help individuals understand their financial health better and enable lenders to make more informed decisions.

================================================================================
WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT
================================================================================

1. HYBRID SCORING APPROACH
   Traditional credit scores only look at your credit history. InsightScore combines:
   - CIBIL Score (40%): Your traditional credit bureau score
   - Behavioral Score (60%): AI-analyzed financial behavior patterns
   
   This gives a more complete picture of your creditworthiness.

2. AI-POWERED ANALYSIS
   We use Google's Gemini AI to analyze your financial behavior across six key dimensions:
   - Income Stability: How consistent and reliable your income is
   - Spending Discipline: Your ability to control expenses
   - Savings Behavior: Your savings patterns and financial cushion
   - Payment Discipline: Your track record of paying bills on time
   - Digital Behavior: Your adoption of digital payment methods
   - Lifestyle Stability: Your financial stability and predictability

3. DOCUMENT-BASED VERIFICATION
   For the most accurate score, upload your official documents:
   - CIBIL Credit Report
   - Bank Statements
   - UPI Transaction History
   - Salary Slips
   
   Our system extracts key metrics and provides verified behavioral analysis.

4. PRIVACY FIRST
   - All documents are processed locally
   - Files are deleted immediately after analysis
   - No raw document data is sent to AI models
   - Only structured metrics are analyzed
   - Your data is secure and private

5. FREE & NO CREDIT IMPACT
   - Check your InsightScore completely free
   - No impact on your credit score
   - No hidden charges
   - Instant results

================================================================================
SCORING METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

BEHAVIORAL SCORE COMPONENTS:

1. Income Stability (15% weight)
   Measures the consistency and reliability of your income sources.

2. Spending Discipline (20% weight)
   Evaluates how well you manage expenses relative to income.

3. Savings Behavior (20% weight)
   Assesses your ability to save and build financial reserves.

4. Payment Discipline (25% weight) - Most Important
   Tracks your history of paying bills and obligations on time.

5. Digital Behavior (10% weight)
   Analyzes your adoption of digital payment methods and transaction patterns.

6. Lifestyle Stability (10% weight)
   Measures financial predictability and stability in spending patterns.

FINAL SCORE:
Your InsightScore combines your CIBIL score (if available) with your behavioral score to give you a comprehensive credit assessment ranging from 300-900, similar to traditional credit scores but more complete.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Is InsightScore free?
A: Yes, checking your InsightScore is completely free with no hidden charges.

Q: Will checking my score affect my credit rating?
A: No, checking your InsightScore has zero impact on your credit score.

Q: How accurate is InsightScore?
A: Verified Score (with documents) provides the most accurate assessment. Quick Score (self-reported) gives a good estimate.

Q: What documents do I need?
A: For Verified Score, you can upload: CIBIL report, Bank statement, UPI transactions, and Salary slip. All are optional but more documents = more accurate score.

Q: How is my data protected?
A: All documents are deleted immediately after processing. Only structured metrics are analyzed. We follow industry-standard security practices.

Q: Can lenders see my score?
A: Only if you share your unique user ID with them. You have full control over who can view your verified score.

Q: How often should I check my score?
A: We recommend checking monthly to track improvements. Our system sends reminders every 30 days.

Q: What's the difference between Quick Score and Verified Score?
A: Quick Score uses self-reported data for fast analysis. Verified Score uses official documents for more accurate, comprehensive assessment.
